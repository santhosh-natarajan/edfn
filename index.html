About Us

Welcome to EDFN Technologies, a pioneering semiconductor and PCB design company based in the vibrant city of Coimbatore, India. At EDFN Technologies, we are passionate about transforming ideas into cutting-edge electronic solutions that power the future. With a deep commitment to innovation and excellence, we are here to revolutionize the semiconductor and PCB design landscape.

Our Journey and Mission :

Founded in 2023, EDFN Technologies was born out of a shared vision among a group of engineers and designers. Our mission is clear to provide world-class semiconductor and PCB design services that empower businesses and individuals to bring their electronic dreams to life. We thrive on challenges, using every project as an opportunity to showcase our expertise and push the boundaries of what's possible.

What sets us apart is our unwavering dedication to quality, precision, and creativity. With a team of highly skilled professionals, we specialize in translating concepts into tangible electronic solutions that exceed expectations. Our deep understanding of semiconductor technologies and PCB design intricacies enables us to deliver designs that are not just functional, but also optimized for performance, manufacturability, and reliability.

Our Services and Solutions

At EDFN Technologies, we offer a comprehensive suite of services and solutions in semiconductor and PCB design, tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our expertise and commitment to excellence ensure that your electronic projects are brought to life with precision, innovation, and reliability.

PCB Design

Our PCB design services focus on creating layouts that maximize functionality, minimize interference, and optimize manufacturability. We create PCBs that serve as robust foundations for your electronic systems.

Mechanical Design

Seamless integration of mechanical design and electronic software by experts, using advanced tools for optimal collaboration. Elevate projects with precision, efficiency, and innovation, engineering robust foundations for electronic systems.

Library Development

Revolutionize electronic projects with our curated libraries—efficiency, accuracy, and compatibility for innovation, empowering your projects.

ATE Solutions

Elevate testing with our Advanced Test Equipment solutions. Our electric and electronic software streamlines processes for precision, efficiency, and reliability, meeting industry standards with cutting-edge technology.

Turn Key Projects

Seamless turnkey solutions: Elevate projects with our expertise, integrating cutting-edge software with advanced electronics for precision and efficiency.

PCB Assembly

Create PCB layouts, design electronic circuits, and simulate electrical systems using specialized software for seamless PCB assembly and optimal functionality.

Fabrication

Creating electronic and electric software involves designing, coding, testing, and optimizing circuits for seamless functionality and reliable performance.

IOT solution

IoT solutions integrate electronic and electric software to enable seamless communication between devices, collect data, and optimize processes, fostering a connected and efficient ecosystem for various applications.

EDA conversation

Explore and analyze electronic and electric systems with EDA Conversion software, facilitating efficient design, simulation, and optimization for seamless integration and performance enhancement.
